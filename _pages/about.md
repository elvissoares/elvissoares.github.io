I'm a 33-years-old physicist living at Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. I'm working as a PostDoc Researcher @ [ATOMS](http://atoms.peq.coppe.ufrj.br/en/index.html) group. 

Currently, my research interests include Statistical Physics of Complex Systems including Liquid Theory, Electrolytes, Colloid, Pattern Formation, and Inhomogeneous Systems. 

Previously, I did my Ph.D. in Physics at [IF-UFRJ](https://www.if.ufrj.br/en/) under the supervision of Prof. J.R.T. de Mello Neto and prof. Takeshi Kodama. My Ph.D Thesis was entitled [Non-equilibrium Phenomena in Thermonuclear Supernovae: from life to death of white dwarf stars](https://github.com/elvissoares/elvissoares.github.io/blob/master/files/Soares2017-PhDThesis.pdf). 

Here you can find my recent publications, PhD thesis, open source projects, and some blog posts on different topics of science.
